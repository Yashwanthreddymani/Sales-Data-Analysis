# 📊 Sales Data Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing restaurant sales data to identify customer behavior, sales trends, and profit-driving factors using **Python, Excel, and Power BI**.

The dataset was cleaned, processed, and transformed into an interactive dashboard to generate business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

The analysis includes:

- Monthly sales trends
- Category-wise sales analysis
- Hourly sales performance
- Weekday sales patterns
- Top customer analysis
- Business recommendations

---

## 🎯 Objectives

The main objectives of this project are:

- Analyze sales performance over time
- Identify high-performing product categories
- Discover customer purchasing behavior
- Understand peak sales hours
- Improve restaurant profitability using data insights

## 📈 Key Insights

### 1. Monthly Sales Trends
- Sales peaked in **April and July**
- Lower sales observed during **May–June and August**

### 2. Sales by Category
- **Main Course** generated the highest revenue
- Desserts and starters also contributed significantly

### 3. Hourly Sales Analysis
- Peak sales occurred during **evening hours**
- Dinner time generated maximum revenue

### 4. Weekday Sales
- Weekend sales performed better
- Friday–Sunday showed higher customer activity

### 5. Top Customers
- Loyal customers contributed significantly to revenue
- Customer retention can improve profitability

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

- Focus promotions during evening hours
- Introduce seasonal offers during low-sales months
- Promote best-selling food categories
- Implement customer loyalty programs
- Increase weekend promotions
